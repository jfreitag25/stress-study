Frequently Asked Questions

Go to the “Create A Study” page, and complete the form on the page to create your own Study ID.

The Study ID lets you keep track of your data and participants for each study you run. This is especially helpful if you are running multiple studies. Let participants sign up to the right study by telling them the study ID. They will need to type this in when creating an account for the app.

  • Download a zip file from THIS LINK. Once downloaded, unzip the file by double clicking on it. A new folder under the name “stress_extension” should appear, which is your unzipped file.
  • Go to chrome://extensions (Note: we need to use chrome! Please install Chrome if you do not have it.)
  • On the top right corner of the screen in the chrome extensions page, turn on “developer mode.”
  • Click on “load unpacked.”
  • Upload the unzipped “stress_extension” folder inside. Move to another chrome tab and refresh your page.
  • An icon will appear on the bottom right corner of your screen. Click on the icon, and once the extension pops up, click on the “log in” link to log in or create an account as instructed.
  • You can completely turn off the app when a workshop is not in session by going back to chrome://extensions, and toggling it off / on.
